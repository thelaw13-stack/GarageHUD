The reasoning layer that interprets GarageHUD memory and advises the owner.

Garage-level health, readiness, risk, market context, and acquisition fit.

Identify vehicles that fit the fleet ecosystem rather than merely matching a search filter.

Preserve a vehicle's story for sale, insurance, valuation, or legacy.
